Messi

Lionel Messi

Argentine footballer

Lionel Andres Messi is a professional football player who played as a forward for the Argentina national team and Spanish club FC Barcelona and will now play for Paris Saint-Germain. Born on 24 June 1987 in Rosario, Argentina, Messi is considered as one of the best footballers of this generation and greatest of all time.

Captain: The Argentine National Team

Club Paris Saint-Germain

Messi started playing for Argentina club Newell's Old Boys at the age of eight. He was a part of the famous ‘Machine of ‘87’ team at Newell’s but was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency at the age of 10. He was then scouted by Spanish club FC Barcelona and after completing his treatment- courtesy Barcelona- at the age of 14, Messi started playing for the club’s youth teams.
